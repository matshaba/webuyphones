Our Story

WeBuyPhones was founded in 2019 in Warsaw, Poland with a simple mission: make premium technology accessible to everyone while reducing electronic waste. What started as a small electronics shop has grown into one of Poland's most trusted pre-owned device marketplaces.

We believe everyone deserves access to quality technology without the premium price tag. By giving devices a second life, we're not just saving our customers money—we're helping build a more sustainable future for our planet.

Today, we operate both online and through our physical retail location in Warsaw, serving customers across Poland and the European Union. Every device that passes through our doors undergoes rigorous inspection by certified technicians, ensuring you receive only the highest quality products.

Our Environmental Impact

The electronics industry is one of the fastest-growing waste streams globally. By extending the life of devices, we're making a real difference:

♻️

50,000+ Devices Saved

Over 50,000 devices prevented from ending up in landfills, extending their useful life by an average of 3-4 years.

🌱

Reduced Carbon Footprint

Manufacturing new devices creates massive CO2 emissions. Each reused device saves approximately 70kg of CO2 compared to buying new.

💧

Water Conservation

Manufacturing one smartphone uses ~13,000 liters of water. By reusing devices, we've saved millions of liters.

⚡

Energy Savings

Refurbishing and reusing devices requires 95% less energy than manufacturing new ones from raw materials.
