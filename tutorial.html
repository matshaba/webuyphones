Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers

Are all devices genuine and original?
Yes, all devices sold on WeBuyPhones are 100% genuine and authentic. We verify every device's IMEI and serial number to ensure authenticity. We do not sell refurbished devices with aftermarket parts.
How do you test devices?
Every device undergoes a comprehensive 50-point inspection by certified technicians. We test display, battery, cameras, buttons, biometrics, connectivity, speakers, microphone, and more. Full diagnostic reports are kept for every device.
What if I receive a faulty device?
Contact us immediately if you receive a faulty device. We'll provide a prepaid return label and either send a replacement device or issue a full refund. Our 12-month warranty covers all hardware defects.
Can I visit your physical store?
Yes! We have a physical retail store in Warsaw, Poland where you can browse devices, get expert advice, and make purchases in person. Visit our About page for store location and hours.
Do you accept broken or damaged devices?
Yes, we buy devices in all conditions including broken, cracked screens, or water damaged. The offered price will reflect the device's condition. Contact us for a quote on damaged devices.
How long does the selling process take?
From submission to payment typically takes 5-7 business days. This includes shipping time (2-3 days), inspection (1-2 days), and payment processing (1-2 days). Express options available.
When will I receive payment for my device?
Once your device passes inspection and is approved, payment is processed within 24 hours. Funds arrive via your chosen method: PayPal (instant), bank transfer (1-2 days), or check (5-7 days).
Is my personal data removed from devices?
Absolutely. Every device undergoes professional data wiping using military-grade software. We ensure all previous owner data is permanently and securely erased before resale. We follow GDPR compliance standards.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, PayPal, bank transfers, and BLIK (for Polish customers). All payments are processed securely with encryption.
Can I cancel my order?
Yes, you can cancel your order before it ships for a full refund. Once shipped, you can still return the device within 30 days under our money-back guarantee.
Do devices come unlocked?
Most of our devices are factory unlocked and work with all carriers. If a device is carrier-locked, this will be clearly stated in the product description before purchase.
What's included with each device?
Each device comes with a compatible charging cable. Original boxes and accessories are included when available (indicated in product listing). All devices come in protective packaging.
How can I contact customer support?
Email us at support@webuyphones.com, call +48 22 123 4567, or visit our physical store in Warsaw. Our support team is available Monday-Friday 9:00-18:00 CET.
